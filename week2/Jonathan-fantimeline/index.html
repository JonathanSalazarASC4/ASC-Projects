Half Life

Half life was released in November , 24 , 1998. It is a science fiction first-person shooter game that
allowed players to preform various of task with total freedom. At the time, the game was considered
a revolutionary on graphics. Half life has earned over fifty PC"Game of the Year" awards and it is
still considered the best game on its genre. Half life has sold over eight million copies following Half-Life 2.

Half Life 2 is the second game of the original game series Half Life by Valve. This was the final chapter of the sequal which striked controversy. Many fans around the world turn this event into an internet sensation. Half Life 2 is a huge step up to the first game. The graphics were enhanced and the ability for total freedom was not restricted. The game was filled with easter eggs that even today dedicated players couldn't find.
